About Me

Growing up as an introvert, I lacked the courage to meet new friends or join any activities during my time at elementary school. I started gaming and soon got addicted. Noticing my deterioration in the interest to learn, my parents decided to send me to study abroad in Beijing. It is the two years of resting and reflection that I realized I was given a second chance. That is when I promise to never give up on every opportunity to learn or gain knowledge.

Upon returning to Hong Kong, I became active in extracurriculars at school. The laborious yet inspiring experiences in the orchestra and rugby team trained me to collaborate with others and have to a resilient attitude under any situation. I was also an active member in Scouts that practice leadership skills from a young age. For college, I am currently studying at UC Berkeley, pursuing a degree in Data Science and Economics.
